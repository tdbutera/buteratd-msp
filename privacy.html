Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

ButeraNet Solutions ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us through this website or by email, we may collect:

  • Name, business name, email address, and phone number you provide
  • Information about your network, business needs, or technical environment that you share with us during discovery
  • Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) collected automatically by our hosting provider

2. How We Use Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule discovery calls or assessments
  • Prepare proposals, quotes, and statements of work
  • Deliver contracted services and ongoing managed support
  • Send occasional service-related communications (we do not send marketing email blasts)

3. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties
  • We do not use your information to train AI models or external systems
  • We do not place advertising trackers on this website
  • We do not share client network architecture, credentials, or business details with anyone outside our engagement

4. Data Security

Client information is stored on encrypted local systems and on encrypted cloud backup. Credentials and access tokens are stored in a hardware-backed password manager. We follow the same network security baseline we deploy for clients — VLAN segmentation, firewall hardening, MFA on admin accounts, and patching.

5. Data Retention

We retain client engagement records (proposals, SOWs, network documentation, invoices) for seven years after the end of an engagement, consistent with standard business and tax record requirements. Discovery-call notes from prospects who do not become clients are deleted within 90 days. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

6. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use third-party advertising cookies. Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and DNS provider (Cloudflare) may set technical cookies required for site delivery and basic security. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tracking tools.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to records-retention obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for further communications

To exercise any of these rights, email travis@buteranet.com.

8. Children's Privacy

This site and our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our practices evolve. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change. Material changes will be communicated to active clients in writing.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email travis@buteranet.com.
