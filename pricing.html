How Pricing Works

One-time project fee

Hardware at vendor cost (transparent — you see the line items) plus labor at the rate published in our rate card. Hawaii GET added at the prevailing rate. 50% deposit at SOW execution; balance at acceptance.

Monthly retainer

Flat monthly amount based on the tier you elect. Includes everything in the comparison table above. No usage-based billing inside your tier — predictable from month to month.

What's never extra

Documentation, status reports, quarterly reviews, configuration backups, software patches, threat-detection-feed updates, and remote support during business hours are included in every tier — they are not add-ons.

What's billed if it happens

Out-of-scope work (e.g., a major change request, after-hours emergency for non-Premier clients, AI token usage above tier threshold) is billed at the rate in our rate card and previewed before any work begins.

Discounts

Standard discounts available — stackable where eligible:

  • Nonprofit / 501(c)(3): 10–15% off project labor + retainer (requires IRS letter)
  • Faith community: 10–20% off project labor + retainer
  • Multi-year retainer (24-month): 10% off retainer (paid annually in advance)
  • Referral from existing client: 5% off project labor
  • Pro bono: Selected strategic engagements only — see our terms

Frequently Asked

Is the assessment really free?

Yes. The 30-minute discovery call and the 60-90 minute on-site assessment are both free. You receive a written design and a fixed-price proposal within 48 hours of the visit. No payment is due until you sign a Statement of Work.

Do you require a long-term contract?

No. Managed services renew monthly until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice. The optional 24-month retainer discount exists for clients who want price stability, but it is opt-in.

What happens to our hardware if we leave?

Tier 1 hardware is yours — you bought it. Tier 2/3 hardware is leased as part of the subscription; if you leave, you have the option to purchase the hardware at depreciated value or return it.

How do AI tokens work?

Tier 3 includes a monthly token threshold appropriate to typical organizational use. If you exceed it, the overage is billed at the per-1000-token rate listed in our rate card — surfaced in your monthly status report so there are no surprises.

Can I move between tiers?

Yes. Tier upgrades and downgrades happen at month boundaries with 30 days notice. Tier 1 → Tier 2 requires hardware deployment and one cutover day. Tier 2 → Tier 3 is software-only and typically completed in a single business day.
